I'm Alice Yuan Zhang, an artist, designer, and program organizer based in Los Angeles, with skills in art direction, graphic design, web design and coding, 3D modeling, AR/VR environments, and strategy consulting.

I deeply believe in the power of creative projects that are collaborative, playful, and functional. My current projects are thematically anchored in generative networks, inter-species collaborations, IRL + URL space-making, magical realism and futuring, and object-oriented ontology.

I am the co-founder of virtual care lab, a 2020-21 resident artist at CultureHub, facilitator of Digital Matterealities research group with NAVEL, and a graduate of University of California, Berkeley. I have given talks at USC Media Arts + Practice, Digital Art Month NYC, and 3HD Festival Berlin.


You can reach me by email or Instagram.
