I'm Alice Yuan Zhang, an artist, designer, and program organizer based in Los Angeles, with skills in graphic design, web design and coding, 3D modeling, and AR/VR environments.

I deeply believe in the power of creative projects that are collaborative, playful, and functional. My current projects are thematically anchored in generative networks, inter-species collaborations, IRL + URL space-making, magical realism and futuring, and object-oriented ontology.

I co-organize virtual care lab and recently facilitated Digital Matterealities, an experimental learning group with NAVEL, and studied at University of California, Berkeley.
